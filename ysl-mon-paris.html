YSL Mon Paris

YSL Mon Paris is a fruity and floral fragrance for women. It features notes of strawberry, raspberry, and pear, with a heart of peony, jasmine, and orange blossom. The base notes include patchouli, white musk, and cedarwood.

This fragrance is perfect for women who want a modern and romantic scent.
