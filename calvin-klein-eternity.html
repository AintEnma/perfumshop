Calvin Klein Eternity

Calvin Klein Eternity is a modern and elegant fragrance for women. It features notes of freesia, sage, and mandarin orange, with a heart of lily, jasmine, and rose. The base notes include sandalwood, amber, and musk, creating a timeless and sophisticated scent.

Ideal for both day and evening wear, Eternity embodies lasting love and commitment.
