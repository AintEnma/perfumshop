Calvin Klein CK One

CK One by Calvin Klein is a fresh and clean unisex fragrance. It features notes of pineapple, green notes, mandarin orange, papaya, bergamot, cardamom, and lemon. The heart includes nutmeg, violet, orris root, jasmine, lily-of-the-valley, and rose. The base notes are amber, musk, cedar, and sandalwood.

This fragrance is perfect for everyday wear and is known for its light, refreshing scent.
