Versace Eros

Versace Eros is a passionate and fresh fragrance for men. It features notes of mint, lemon, and green apple, with a heart of tonka bean, ambroxan, and geranium. The base notes include vanilla, cedarwood, and oakmoss.

This fragrance is perfect for men who want a bold and seductive scent.
