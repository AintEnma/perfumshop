Versace Bright Crystal

Versace Bright Crystal is a fresh and floral fragrance for women. It features notes of pomegranate, yuzu, and frosted accord, with a heart of peony, magnolia, and lotus flower. The base notes include amber, musk, and mahogany.

This fragrance is perfect for women who want a light, elegant, and refreshing scent.
