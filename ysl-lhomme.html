YSL L'Homme

YSL L'Homme is a fresh and woody fragrance for men. It features notes of ginger, bergamot, and lemon, with a heart of violet leaf and basil. The base notes include cedarwood, vetiver, and tonka bean.

This fragrance is perfect for men who want a clean and sophisticated scent.
